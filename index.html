My Story

I was born at Pembury Hospital, and lived in Hildenbourgh,Hillinden Rise and then Seal road. I went to two schools my primary school was Seal C of E school and my secondary school was Bradbourne School, it was an all girls school but then we mixed with the boys school which at the time was called Wilderness. Once we merged our school became known as Knole Academy.
